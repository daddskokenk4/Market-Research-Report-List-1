<p><p>The Carbon Fiber-Reinforced Carbon (C & C) market is primarily influenced by two manufacturing processes: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation. CVD involves depositing carbon from gaseous precursors onto a substrate, creating a robust, lightweight material with high thermal resistance. In contrast, the Liquid Impregnation Process involves infusing a carbon matrix with liquid precursors, which upon polymerization and curing, enhances structural integrity. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including aerospace, automotive, and energy sectors, leveraging their unique properties for advanced performance.</p></p>
