<p><p>The Automotive 4WD parts market encompasses several key components, including two differentials, a transfer case, and other related parts. Two differentials allow for wheel speed variation between the left and right wheels, enhancing traction. The transfer case distributes power from the engine to the front and rear axles, enabling all-wheel drive functionality. Other parts include driveshafts, axles, and locking mechanisms that further improve vehicle performance in off-road conditions. Together, these components enhance vehicle stability and control across various terrains.</p></p>
