<p><p>The 24-hour Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) device market features two primary types: Ordinary ABPM and Mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM devices are traditional, typically consisting of a cuff connected to a monitor that records blood pressure at regular intervals throughout the day and night. In contrast, Mobile-based ABPM integrates advanced technology, allowing users to connect their monitoring devices to smartphones or tablets, enabling real-time data access, analysis, and sharing with healthcare providers, enhancing patient engagement and convenience.</p></p>
