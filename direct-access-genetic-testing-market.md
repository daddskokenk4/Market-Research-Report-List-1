<p><strong>The Direct-Access Genetic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diagnostic Screening</li><li>PGD</li><li>Relationship testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-access genetic testing encompasses various market types including diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening identifies genetic disorders in individuals, facilitating early intervention and management. PGD is used by couples undergoing in vitro fertilization to detect genetic abnormalities in embryos before implantation, ensuring healthier outcomes. Relationship testing, often involving paternity or ancestry checks, evaluates genetic connections between individuals, providing insights into familial ties and genetic heritage. Together, these markets enhance individual health and understanding of genetic relationships.</p></p>
