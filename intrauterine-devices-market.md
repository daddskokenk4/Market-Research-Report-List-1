<p><strong>The Intrauterine Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hormonal IUD</li><li>Copper IUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intrauterine devices (IUD) market comprises two primary types: hormonal IUDs and copper IUDs. Hormonal IUDs release progestin, which thickens cervical mucus and thins the uterine lining, providing effective pregnancy prevention for several years. In contrast, copper IUDs utilize copper's spermicidal properties to prevent fertilization without hormones. Both types are highly effective, long-lasting contraceptive options, appealing to various consumer preferences. The market is influenced by factors such as efficacy, side effects, duration of use, and consumer awareness.</p></p>
