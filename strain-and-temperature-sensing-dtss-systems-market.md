<p><p>Strain and Temperature Sensing (DTSS) systems are crucial for monitoring structural integrity and environmental conditions. The market can be categorized into Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS and Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS. Single Mode Fiber-Based DTSS utilizes a single light path for enhanced sensitivity and precision, making it ideal for long-distance applications. In contrast, Multimode Fiber-Based DTSS accommodates multiple light paths, offering advantages in shorter-distance measurements with easier installation and cost-effectiveness, making both suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
