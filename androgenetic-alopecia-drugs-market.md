<p><p>The androgenetic alopecia drugs market comprises primarily two main types: Finasteride and Minoxidil, along with other treatments. Minoxidil, a topical solution, promotes hair growth by enhancing blood flow to hair follicles. Other treatments may include low-level laser therapy and herbal supplements, which offer alternative options for managing hair loss. Together, these therapies address various aspects of androgenetic alopecia.</p></p>
