<p><p>The aircraft duct couplings market is primarily segmented into two types: thread type and unthreaded type. Thread type couplings feature threaded connections that provide a secure, leak-proof attachment, ideal for high-pressure applications in aircraft systems. Unthreaded type couplings, on the other hand, rely on other mechanisms, such as pin or clamp systems, for connecting ducts, allowing for quicker assembly and maintenance. Each type offers distinct advantages based on the specific requirements of aircraft ducting systems, including durability, ease of installation, and performance reliability.</p></p>
