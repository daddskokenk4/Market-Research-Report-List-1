<p><strong>The Automotive Aluminum Extruded Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Extrusion Type</li><li>Indirect Extrusion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive aluminum extruded parts market comprises two main types: direct extrusion and indirect extrusion. Direct extrusion involves forcing heated aluminum billets through a die to create continuous shapes, commonly used for structural components due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Indirect extrusion, on the other hand, uses a hollow die where the ram pushes the billet into the die cavity, allowing for more complex shapes and better surface finishes. Both types cater to the automotive industry's demand for lightweight, durable components.</p></p>
