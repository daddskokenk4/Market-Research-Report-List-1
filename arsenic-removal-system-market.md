<p><p>The arsenic removal system market encompasses various technologies to eliminate arsenic from water. The "Precipitative Process" involves adding agents to form insoluble arsenic compounds, which can be filtered out. The "Adsorptive Process" uses materials like activated carbon to trap arsenic molecules. "Ion Exchange Process" exchanges arsenic ions with less harmful ions in a resin. The "Membrane Process" involves filtering water through semi-permeable membranes to separate arsenic. "Others" may include new or hybrid technologies offering innovative arsenic remediation solutions.</p></p>
