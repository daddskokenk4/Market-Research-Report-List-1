<p><p>Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) enhances vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during hard braking, allowing for better steering control. The ABS market comprises three primary components: sensors that monitor wheel speed, the Electronic Control Unit (ECU) that processes sensor data and adjusts brake pressure, and the hydraulic unit that modulates brake pressure to each wheel. Together, these components ensure optimal braking performance, reduce stopping distances, and improve overall vehicle stability, making ABS a crucial technology in modern automotive design.</p></p>
