<p><p>The ultrasound needles market includes various types tailored for specific procedures. Aspiration needles (EUS-FNA) are designed for fine-needle aspiration, allowing for the extraction of fluid or tissue samples from lesions for diagnostic purposes. Biopsy needles (EUS-FNB), on the other hand, are used for core needle biopsy, obtaining larger tissue samples for more detailed analysis. Both needle types enhance the accuracy of endoscopic ultrasound procedures, facilitating better diagnosis and treatment planning for conditions affecting internal organs.</p></p>
