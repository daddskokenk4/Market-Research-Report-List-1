<p><p>Adjustable linear regulators are essential components in power management, providing precise voltage control for various applications. The market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel regulators. Single-channel regulators supply power to one output, ideal for simple circuits needing specific voltage levels. In contrast, multi-channel regulators can deliver multiple outputs, catering to complex systems with various voltage requirements. This versatility allows designers to optimize space and efficiency, making these regulators crucial in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and embedded systems.</p></p>
