<p><p>The Methylene Diphenylisocyanate (MDI) market is segmented into three main types: Polymeric MDI, Pure MDI, and Modified MDI. Polymeric MDI is used primarily in polyurethane manufacturing due to its versatility and performance in producing flexible and rigid foams. Pure MDI, characterized by its high purity, is better suited for applications requiring precise chemical properties, such as coatings and adhesives. Modified MDI combines elements of both, enhancing specific properties for specialized applications while retaining some benefits of the other forms.</p></p>
