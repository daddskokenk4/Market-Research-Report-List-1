<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Displacement Sensor Market is categorized into two main types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors rely on physical contact with the object being measured, often providing high precision for displacement measurement, but may be limited by wear and tear. In contrast, non-contact type sensors utilize techniques such as laser, ultrasound, or infrared to measure displacement without direct contact, offering advantages like speed and durability while minimizing the risk of damage to the object.</p></p>
