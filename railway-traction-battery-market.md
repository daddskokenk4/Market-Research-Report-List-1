<p><strong>The Railway Traction Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Lead Acid Battery</li><li>Pure Lead Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li><li>Lithium-Ion Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway traction battery market features several key types, each offering distinct characteristics. Open lead acid batteries are cost-effective but require maintenance due to electrolyte exposure. Pure lead batteries provide improved cycle life and faster charging, ideal for demanding applications. Gel batteries, with their immobilized electrolyte, offer safety and durability, making them suitable for varied environments. Lithium-ion batteries, known for their high energy density and lower weight, are increasingly favored for modern railway systems, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs.</p></p>
