<p><strong>The DNA Microarray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligonucleotide DNA Microarrays (oDNA)</li><li>Complementary DNA Microarrays (cDNA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA microarray market is primarily segmented into two types: Oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) and Complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA). Oligonucleotide DNA microarrays utilize short, synthesized DNA sequences that target specific genes, allowing for high specificity in gene expression analysis. In contrast, cDNA microarrays involve the use of complementary DNA strands synthesized from mRNA, enabling the examination of expressed genes in various biological samples. Both types are integral for genomics research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine applications.</p></p>
