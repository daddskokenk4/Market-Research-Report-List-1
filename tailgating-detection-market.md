<p><strong>The Tailgating Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Imaging Measurement Tech</li><li>Non-Imaging Tech</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tailgating detection encompasses two main market types: imaging measurement technology and non-imaging technology. Imaging measurement tech utilizes advanced cameras and sensors to visually monitor and analyze foot traffic patterns, accurately identifying instances of unauthorized access when individuals closely follow one another. In contrast, non-imaging tech employs methods such as weight sensors, infrared beams, or proximity sensors to detect tailgating without visual input. Both technologies serve to enhance security by preventing unauthorized entry in various settings, including commercial buildings and secure facilities.</p></p>
