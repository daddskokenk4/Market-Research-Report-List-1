<p><ul><li>Dry Erase Whiteboard Markers</li><li>Wet Erase Whiteboard Markers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whiteboard markers market is primarily divided into two types: dry erase and wet erase markers. Dry erase markers are designed for easily erasable writing on whiteboards, featuring low-odor, quick-drying ink, making them ideal for fast-paced settings. Wet erase markers, on the other hand, use a water-based ink that requires a damp cloth for removal, providing more permanence and control, which is beneficial for presentations or detailed illustrations. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in educational and professional environments.</p></p>
