<p><ul><li>Arc Target</li><li>Sputtering Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) targets are essential in thin-film deposition processes. Arc targets utilize an electric arc to vaporize material, particularly metals, creating a high-quality deposition for applications like coatings on tools and decorative finishes. Sputtering targets, on the other hand, involve bombarding a target material with ions, causing ejection of atoms that deposit onto a substrate, ideal for semiconductor and photovoltaic applications. Both target types cater to distinct market needs in industries such as electronics, optics, and surface engineering.</p></p>
