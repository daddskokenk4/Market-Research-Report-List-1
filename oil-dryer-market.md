Vacuum dryers operate under reduced pressure, minimizing temperature impact and preserving oil quality during drying. Air dryers employ airflow to remove moisture, suitable for low-temperature drying. Other technologies may include microwave or infrared dryers, which utilize advanced heating methods. Together, these types cater to diverse needs in oil processing and storage across industries.</p></p>
