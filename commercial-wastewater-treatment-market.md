<p><p>The commercial wastewater treatment market comprises three main types: biological, mechanical, and chemical treatments. Biological treatment utilizes microorganisms to break down organic matter in wastewater, effectively reducing pollutants. Mechanical treatment involves physical processes, such as screening and sedimentation, to remove solids and larger particles. Chemical treatment uses chemicals to neutralize contaminants, precipitate solids, or disinfect water. Each method targets specific types of pollutants and is chosen based on the wastewater composition and desired treatment outcomes.</p></p>
