<p><strong>The Real-time Bidding (RTB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Auction</li><li>Invited Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Real-time bidding (RTB) is a programmatic advertising technology that allows advertisers to bid for ad impressions in real-time through an auction process. In an open auction market, any advertiser can participate, resulting in broader competition and varied bid prices. In contrast, an invited auction market is more exclusive, where only selected advertisers can bid on specific inventory, often leading to higher-quality ads but less competitive pricing. Both types facilitate efficient ad placements based on demand and supply dynamics.</p></p>
