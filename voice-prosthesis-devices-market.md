<p><strong>The Voice Prosthesis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indwelling Voice Prosthesis Devices</li><li>Non-Indwelling Voice Prosthesis Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voice prosthesis devices market is primarily divided into two types: indwelling and non-indwelling voice prosthesis devices. Indwelling voice prostheses are surgically implanted directly into the tracheoesophageal puncture, allowing patients to produce voice sounds more easily over time, with minimal maintenance. In contrast, non-indwelling devices are removable and can be inserted and replaced by the patient or healthcare provider. Both types aim to restore vocal communication for individuals who have undergone laryngectomy or other voice-altering surgeries.</p></p>
